How To Sign Up:

  1. Open start.gg in a browser.
  2. Look up the name of your desired tournament and click on the upcoming one.
  3. Click on the "register" button.
  4. Enter the correct details, such as name, tag, and payment, and you're all set!

Frequently Asked Questions:

What if I want to pay with cash?
There is an option to pay at the venue with cash on start.gg when registering.
What if I can't find the tournament I'm looking for?
The newest tournament page probably isn't up yet. It will go up evenutally unless they are cancelled.
What if I have questions about a specific tournament?
Message the venue or contact information listened on each tournament's start.gg or personal website.
