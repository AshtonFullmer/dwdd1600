Welcome to Utah Smash!

Ever since 2008 with the release of Super Smash Bros. Brawl, the competitive scene in Utah had begun to grow. However, it wasn't until 2014 when Super Smash Bros. for Wii U released, exponentially increasing the competitve growth for the series alongside Super Smash Bros. Melee. Now, with the current generation of Smash, Super Smash Bros. Ultimate, a new generation of players were formed as well, further sementing our scene as Utah Smash.

Home to one of the biggest weekly locals in the country, LAN King, Utah is a very friendly and welcoming community for all players of all ages!

Renowned Players Making Waves

Our scene has a multitude of top players in Smash Ultimate who see results across the world, such as:

  • Apple Reviewer, the #1 Mii Brawler player and Top 100 globally.
